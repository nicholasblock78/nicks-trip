What am I doing?

I'm embarking upon a massive journey this fall. Come Labor Day, I will have finished my classes and physical therapy for my shoulder. My lease also ends in September. Quite literally, I will be a free man. Nothing to hold me back from pursuing my dreams.

Why am I doing this?

Many of you do not know I had a similar type of trip planned a year ago. I was going to fly to San Francisco, interview and see friends, then rent a car and drive up the coast to Seattle. From there, I would fly back. I was going to fly out on Frontier a couple weeks after the first initial Ebola victim flew on a Frontier plane. I was going by myself and I let Ebola be my excuse to not go, even though I didn't want to go alone at the time. This decision came after everything had been booked and airfare paid for. Of course. (I used miles to grab a couple cheap one ways. So no money lost technically, but such a waste.)

This time around I'm ready!

How it came to be

As you'll see below, I used a National Park Road Atlas with tracing paper. I went through with pencil and circled everything I wanted to see. (My goal was to visit warm places plus Boston because these are places I might try to move to.) Once I circled to my heart's content, I simply connected the dots.
